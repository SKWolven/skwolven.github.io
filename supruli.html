VISUAL DESIGN

I've been singing with Supruli, the NYC Georgian Choir, for over 5 years. I designed a set of gig posters for a performance in 2020 as well as social media ready graphics and postcards.

The theme for this poster is based on a traditional Georgian garment called a chokha.

My inspiration for this design was the Georgian landscape and the Caucasus mountains.

"Supruli" means tablecloth so I took my inspiration for this poster, social media graphics, and postcard from Georgian textile patterns and colors.
