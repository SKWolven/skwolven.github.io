What I can do


I have experience with retail, e-commerce, customer service, human resources, operations, and administrative support across a variety of industries. I’ve worked at a toy store, a bar, a chocolate factory, an animal hospital, and an animation production studio. I’m never happy with just one project on my plate, and I love to learn new things. I have a keen editorial eye and am passionate about helping creators realize the best version of their own projects. See below for my work history and writing samples.

Frederator Books is a digital children's book publisher where I perform developmental, structural, copy, and line editing. At least six books that I edited are due to be released by the end of Q3 2019.

With thoughtful editing suggestions I helped one author turn his book idea into a series of three. We worked together to bring consistency to the meter and rhyme scheme, and I corrected grammatical and typographical errors.

I have also written and edited copy for the website.

StashRiot is the e-commerce subsidiary of Frederator Networks. Originally founded to be a one stop hub for limited edition merchandise from a variety of fandoms, it has since become the shop exclusively for merchandise from the world of Frederator. Top brands include Adventure Time, Bravest Warriors, and Bee and PuppyCat. I am currently the only employee of StashRiot, which means I do everything from product design to copywriting and marketing to charge capture and customer service.

Visit StashRiot and check out the Instagram and Facebook pages.

Gas Powered Pin Set
This pin set was designed by an intern under my direction in late 2018 and became a top seller soon after launching in Q1 2019. I'm really pleased with how they turned out and am proud of how well they've been received by fans.

I kicked off the social campaign with a few posts to drum up anticipation before the official announcement.

Since these pins are one of our few in-stock items (as opposed to print on demand) and we only made a limited run, I've been especially focused on conveying urgency in our social media campaigns. I'm anticipating that we will sell out by the end of 2019.

Frederator Networks is a digital media company based in New York City. Frederator Digital also runs the Channel Frederator Network, the largest network for animators, artists, and gamers on YouTube.

  • Regularly write intra-office memos in my role as Office and Facilities manager, and as HR manager.

Park East Animal Hospital is a high-end veterinary practice on the Upper East Side in New York City. They are one of only 12-15% of veterinary practices to acheive AAHA accreditation.

Since I happen to be a tech savvy individual, I was often tasked with learning new practice tecnhologies in order to train the rest of the staff. I wrote numerous technical and process documents over the course of my tenure there from 2007-2015.

As the social media manager I set up the current version of the Park East website, which is largely unchanged since I left. Much of the copy on the site was written or edited by me, like this page about international travel paperwork. I also wrote seasonal blog posts about pet health and safety which were published on the site. And I wrote posts for the Park East Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram accounts, which I created.
