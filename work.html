I love the unique challenge of crafting informative, engaging copy with compassion and empathy in limited space. In order to improve my skills I've signed up with Daily UX Writing. The following are an assortment of prompts and my solutions to them.

This is my version of the Facebook Page creation process. The biggest change is that I eliminated all of the optional steps that they currently include. Facebook makes it so easy to add those elements afterwards that it seems unneccessary to include it here. I also changed the language of the final step from 'visit your Page' to 'Finish Page'. Facebook's current version is ambiguous and a user might think they will still have an opportunity to go back and make changes.
