ABOUT ME

Growing up into the digital age I’ve seen stories evolve and the English language turned on its head over and over again. Who could have predicted we’d be using the word literally to mean figuratively and that our vocabulary would expand to include pictograms? ¯\_(ツ)_/¯ But that’s the beauty of language. It’s always evolving, and unexpected trends are the norm. Especially now, because internet.
If you're interested in how words can improve your product's user experience, let's talk!

My approach My story My interests

I believe that good writing begins with empathy. The ability to understand the feelings and motivations of the audience Is the only way to consistently deliver engaging copy.

It continues with empathy. The ability to understand the personality and goals of your business or product is the best way to internalize the brand voice and convey the right message every time.

And it ends with (you guessed it!) empathy. An awareness of language and how people use it, how it makes them feel, is essential. Words do not exist in a vacuum. A good writer needs to understand context to put all of these elements together to bring the right message to the right people.

I have been a language nerd for almost as long as I can remember. From creating a secret alphabet with my best friend in 3rd grade, to learning Esperanto for fun in high school, to studying Ancient Greek and Latin in College, I've spent a lot of time thinking about words and grammar.

My experience working in customer service, office administration, and human resources has allowed me to practice creating positive experiences in the real world. Digital user experience is the same in a lot of ways.

With UX writing I am able to combine my passion for language with my expertise in optimizing user and customer experiences.

Here I am passing my love of language on to a new generation.
